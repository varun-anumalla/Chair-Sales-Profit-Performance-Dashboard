A **Power BI Data Analytics Project** that provides in-depth insights into the sales, profit, and customer behavior of a chair manufacturing company.  
This interactive dashboard helps track KPIs, identify top-performing products, and understand sales trends across regions and years.  

---

## üìä Project Overview  
The goal of this project was to design a **visually appealing, data-driven dashboard** that enables better decision-making for business performance tracking.  

Through this dashboard, users can:  
- Monitor total sales, profit, and units sold.  
- Identify top-selling chair models.  
- Analyze customer purchase patterns across cities.  
- Observe yearly and monthly sales trends.  
- Compare profits and COGS to measure overall business health.  

---

## üéØ Problem Statement  
The company wanted a centralized dashboard to track **sales performance, product-wise profitability, and regional growth**.  
Manually tracking sales data made it difficult to identify trends and profitable product lines quickly.  

This Power BI dashboard provides a **complete data story** through interactive visuals and key metrics.
